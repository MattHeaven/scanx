At-Home Testing

Screening and Clinical Trials





During the COVID-19 pandemic, we saw that at-home, point of need testing was not only possible, but convenient and safe for patients. And yet, for clinical trials, screening, pre-procedure testing, and regular monitoring, we are still requiring patients to attend their doctor’s office or local hospital for testing.

There’s a better way - we provide solutions that allow patients to test in the comfort, convenience, and privacy of their own homes. Digitalised testing with our ClearScreen solution means clinicians can be assured the test was performed correctly and the results, test images, and analysis are instantly available for the clinician to review in our web portal. ClearScreen can accommodate a variety of test types and procedures. Results can be easily integrated into electronic health records or other clinical systems.

YORKSURe Case Study:

  • 

  • Funded by Yorkshire Cancer Research. Coordinated by Cancer Research UK & King’s College London Cancer Prevention Trials Unit

  • 

  • Study to determine if bladder cancer can be identified through at-home testing using a urine dip test; > 60s patients are the primary group

  • 

  • 6000 patients from 3 cohorts for the initial pilot; 100,000 when pilot is expanded nationwide

  • 

  • ScanX provided much-needed technology as well as the physical tests to a study that did not have a solution in place to record results. We created a branded app and a customised test flow that met all their requirements. Also provided an analytics environment for research nurses to view test results and follow up with patients
