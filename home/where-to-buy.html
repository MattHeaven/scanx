Ask for ScanX at Your Local Pharmacy

ScanX is now stocked at hundreds of community pharmacies across the UK.

Many of these offer a Test & Treat service, where they will provide prescription medication if you have a positive test result. If they don’t have ScanX in stock, they can usually receive it same-day, so you can get tested and get the treatment you need faster.
